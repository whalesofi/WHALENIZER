03
Technical Mechanics

The bonding curve
is the market.

Every user profile has shares governed by an automated market maker bonding curve. No order books. No market makers. No price manipulation. Pure mathematical price discovery.

SHARE PRICE FORMULA
P(n) = P₀ × 1.5ⁿ
Where P₀ is the base price, n is the current share supply, and 1.5 is the price multiplier applied on every purchase. Every buy increases the price. Every sell decreases it.

Every share purchase mints a new share and increases the price by 1.5×. Every share sale burns an existing share and decreases the price accordingly. This creates exponential price growth that rewards early believers and makes wash trading immediately and provably unprofitable — every round-trip trade results in a net loss.

FEE STRUCTURE

Every profile share transaction generates a 5% protocol fee collected automatically by the smart contract, split equally between the platform treasury and the profile owner being traded.

2.5%
Platform Treasury — sustains protocol development and ecosystem growth
2.5%
Profile Owner — direct on-chain revenue for every creator being traded

WALLET ARCHITECTURE

Every new user receives a deterministic non-custodial EVM wallet generated from a cryptographic hash of their verified email and a server-side secret seed. Compatible with Ethereum and Base, exportable to any EVM app. Users own their keys. WHALENIZER never has unilateral access to user funds.

Security

Security first.
Launch second.

No mainnet deployment on Ethereum or Base will occur before a formal third-party security audit is completed. This is not a guideline — it is an absolute constraint.

The catastrophic security exploit that destroyed Stars Arena within days of launch is the most important cautionary example in SocialFi history and WHALENIZER will never repeat that mistake.

CURRENT SECURITY MEASURES

The existing platform implements OTP email verification preventing bot registrations, SHA-256 password hashing, HTTPS enforcement across all traffic, environment variable credential management, parameterized database queries preventing SQL injection, and admin dashboard access control via server-side authentication.

AUDIT TIMELINE

M1
Core Trading Contracts Audit
Bonding curve, share minting and burning, and fee distribution contracts audited before Ethereum and Base mainnet deployment.
M2
Rewards Contracts Audit
Follow-up audit of all on-chain rewards distribution and points conversion contracts before rewards system launch.
M3
Final Comprehensive Audit
Complete audit of all contracts including governance system before community governance transfer and token launch.

BUG BOUNTY PROGRAM

A public bug bounty program launches simultaneously with mainnet deployment offering rewards from $100 for low-severity findings up to $10,000 for critical vulnerabilities, creating a continuous community-driven security layer beyond the formal audit period.
